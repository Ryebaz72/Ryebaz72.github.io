Keyword Research

Keyword Research is an extremely important activity. If done properly you will be able to target the specific terms that your potential customers are searching for. This will enable you to appear at the top of the listings, positioning your website right in front of your future customers when they are in the mood to buy.

You need to decide if a particular keyword phrase is actually searched for before using it on your website. You want to target keywords that have a high enough search volume, with preferably lower levels of competition. Your keyword research should help you to decide this.

If you are not sure how to carry out the research needed, or if you just don’t have the time, then we can help you. We can put together a suitable package for your needs. It doesn’t matter whether you are starting from scratch or have an existing site that you would like to change. We can analyse your competitors, to see what keywords they are targeting. Then provide a report showing alternative keywords, and likely monthly search volumes. We will also tell you how competitive these keywords are, so that you can decide if you would like to target them.
